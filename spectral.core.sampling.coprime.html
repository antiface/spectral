spectral.core.sampling.coprime module¶

class spectral.core.sampling.coprime.Coprime(a, b)[source]¶

Bases: spectral.core.sampling.multicoset.MultiCoset

Coprime coset sampler implementation. Derives from the multi-coset class and implements its own (coprime) intervals. These intervals are based on two samplers with Fs1/Fs2 = a/b with a and b the coprime numbers.

Parameters:
  • a – First coprime number
  • b – Second coprime number
coprime_multiples(a, b)[source]¶

Generator function that generates a integer multiples of the coprime numbers starting with zero (in order of size) until all multiples smaller than a * b are returned

Parameters:
  • a – first coprime number
  • b – second coprime number
Yields:

x in ((n*a) union (n*b)) for n in [0, 1, ...] and x < a * b
