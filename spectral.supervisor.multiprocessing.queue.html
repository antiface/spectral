spectral.supervisor.multiprocessing.queue module¶

class spectral.supervisor.multiprocessing.queue.SafeQueue(size=10)[source]¶

Safe Queue implementation is a wrapper around standard multiprocessing queue. Implements safe queuing and dequeueing.

Parameters:size – Optional argument that defines size of queue (default is 10).

Method that wraps around get. Implements a lock to ensure race conditions are avoided.
