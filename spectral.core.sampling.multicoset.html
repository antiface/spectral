spectral.core.sampling.multicoset module¶

class spectral.core.sampling.multicoset.MultiCoset(intervals, N, M)[source]¶

Bases: spectral.core.sampling.sampling.Sampler

Multi coset sampler implementation. Class that converts uniformly sampled signals into aperiodically sampled ones.

Parameters:
  • intervals – Intervals that the multi-coset sampler samples at.
  • N – The downsampling factor.
  • M – Number of devices.
